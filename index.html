Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does early rot detection work?

We use a specialized AI model called YOLOv11 trained on thousands of onion images. It detects subtle changes in skin texture.

Do farmers need internet?

No, continuous internet is not required. The system works offline and stores data locally.

How accurate is the model?

Our current field tests show a 98% accuracy rate in detecting common onion rot types.

Can it work in extreme heat?

Yes. The bamboo structure naturally regulates temperature, and electronics are heat-shielded.
